Why this industry is attractive

High-value, bulky delivery means a poor doorstep experience can directly affect brand perception and repeat purchase behavior.

Furniture and appliance delivery often requires two-person handling, appointment windows, installation and removal workflows.

Manufacturers bypassing traditional channels need delivery visibility and orchestration capabilities that support direct customer relationships.

Bulky goods, regional coverage and installation teams make dynamic routing and capacity planning a strategic requirement.
